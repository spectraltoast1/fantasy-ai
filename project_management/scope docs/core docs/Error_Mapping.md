## A List of common error types this project will account for

### WAIVERS ###
- overvaluing a one-week spike
- undervaluing opportunity growth with lagging fantasy production
- overvaluing fantasy production from big plays
- anchor bias of droppable players
- overspending FAAB
- making FAAB budget calls without considering opponent budget and need
- unjustified churn (a move that serves no nameable strategic reason)

### START/SIT ###
- overthinking a matchup and benching a stud player
- not accouning for leage/matchup context for flex spot decisions (ceiling vs floor)
- starting a player based off a spike week

### TRADES ###
- not reading the other team's need leading to trade offers that rarely get accepted
- not reading your own team's needs leading to trades you shouldn't accept or trades you should make but don't
- overvaluing your own players making negotiations harder
